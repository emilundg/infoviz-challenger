Risk of Critical O-ring Failure

21 jan 1986

Important!

Abstract: Temperature of the O-ring is directly affecting the risk for blow-by, which means the leakage of gas past the piston of an engine at maximum pressure. At around 50F blow-by could be experienced in case joints.

Possible consequences: Failure of O-ring leading to leakage and possibly explosion.

Conclusion: The Challenger should not launch at 28-01-1986.

Data supporting the claim

Below different relevant data can be found that supports the conclusion and possible consequences above.
